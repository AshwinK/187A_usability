User Control and Freedom


"Users often choose system functions by mistake and will need a clearly marked "emergency exit" to leave the unwanted state without having to go through an extended dialogue. Support undo and redo." - Nielsen


This site seriously has to re-work its information and content so that it gives users more control. As a user, I couldn't tell when I would go to another site or just another page on the lousiana.edu site. If you do traverse outside of the main louisiana.edu page, there is really no way to get back even if you are on branched site such as the Academic Success site.

Let's say I am on the Current Students page, there are a huge amount of links that go to outside sites with no way to return to the home page. I really can't emphasize my dislike for the Academic Support tab as a result of this. There are multiple links outside of the louisiana.edu, which lead to pages that are without browser support, difficult to return from. The Athletics page has the same issue, its difficult to return from the actual Ragin Cajun's page.
